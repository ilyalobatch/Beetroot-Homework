About Us

Noquo Foods

We love cheese. We love yoghurt. Ice cream too, and so many other animal based products that are fundamental to our culture and heritage. But using animals to produce these goodies is extremely wasteful and destructive.

In early 2019 we, Sorosh Tavakoli and Anja Leissner, started Noquo Foods to challenge the status quo andre-invent the foods we love, starting with cheese. Read more in blog (‘blog’ links to:https://medium.com/@noquofoods).
